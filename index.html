Connect with Pride@Kellogg While Visiting Campus

Choosing where and whether to go to business school is a profoundly personal decision. Pride@Kellogg is happy to connect interested prospective students with current members so applicants can learn first-hand about the queer experience at Kellogg.

Building Professional Success In and Out of the Classroom

Connecting the out business leaders of today and tomorrow, Pride@Kellogg sends a large delegation of current students and alumni to the Reaching Out MBA Conference every year. Find out more about how you can connect with P@K at the next Reaching Out event.

A Vibrant Alumni Network That Spans the Globe

The bonds we make at Kellogg persist on after graduation. Kellogg has a proud history being at the forefront of its peer schools in friendliness to LGBTQ+ people. This history has allowed us to build a diverse alumni network helps keep Pride@Kellogg strong.
