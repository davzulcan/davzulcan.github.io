Brooklyn

Located southeast of Manhattan, Brooklyn is known in the popoular imagination as the haven of hipsters and post-industrial housing. An enormous borough, millennials tend to concentrate in the neighbourhoods closest to Manhattan. Some neighbourhoods to consider in your search include:

  • Bedford-Stuyvesant
  • Brooklyn Heights
  • Crown Heights
  • Downtown Brooklyn
  • Park Slope
  • Williamsburg

These are just some of the many places where you can look. Depending on your willingness to trade price vs. proximity to Manhattan, you can find some great communities in deep Brooklyn.
